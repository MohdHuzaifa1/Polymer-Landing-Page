Everything you need to grow your team

Instant job board

You'll have a branded job board as a centralized place to display all the open positions at your organization.

Candidate management

As they apply, new candidates automatically show up in the Polymer hiring dashboard for efficient review and management.

Team collaboration

Hiring is a team sport and Polymer is set up to support it. With no limit on your user-count, you can invite all the help you need.

Manage the entire hiring workflow

Polymer's built with productivity at heart and is loaded with features to help you hire more effectively. It's quick to learn, fast to navigate, and empowering to use.

  • Flexible applicant tracking
  • Customizable workflows
  • Rich candidate profiles
  • Built-in candidate messaging
  • Bulk messaging
  • Message templates
  • Shared notes & documentation

Keep track of everything

A robust notification system ensures you'll never miss a thing. You'll have a clear overview of everything that happens within your hiring teams.

Work together

Polymer is set up to allow everyone on your team to contribute to the hiring effort. With features like user roles and job assignments, you can easily divvy up the work and get everyone involved.

Tailor your experience

Polymer's customizations go deep. Make it yours.

Dark mode enabled

With support for both light and dark themes, Polymer fits in perfectly with the rest of your productivity suite.

Jobs API included

You can either use our ready-made job board or build your own front-end using our developer API.
